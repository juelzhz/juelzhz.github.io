About Me!

My name is Julian Rojas and I am a teacher at Sun Valley Magnet School. I was born and raised in San Diego and studied Mathematics at UCLA. During my Junior year, I applied for a Joint Math/Education Program where I then graduated with a Bachelors in Math in 2013 and a Masters in Education in 2014. I have taught 8th grade Math, Algebra 1, Introduction to Engineering, Principles of Engineering and will now be teaching Computer Science Principles. I have been involved with music ever since I was in the 3rd grade and am currently teaching myself how to play the guitar. I have recently discovered a new hobby of mine which is running and will be running my third LA Marathon in 2017. I also have a cat which I naturally named "Pythagoras." (Her image is found below.)
